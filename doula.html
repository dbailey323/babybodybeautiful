2 Antenatal Visits

Our opportunity for us to connect further and discuss anything on your mind pre birth. We will go over any questions or concerns, chat about your birth preferences and help with writing them down. we will discuss how I can best support you (and your birth partner if you are having one) during your labour and birth. This is also great if you are taking any other courses to help you with birth (ie hypnobirthing) these sessions last up to 2 hours from the comfort of your home.

On Call from 38 - 42 weeks

My phone will be on night and day ready to support your labour and birth whether it may be spontaneous or planned. You can call anytime in this period when you think labour has started and I will be with you as soon as possible to provide support. This can be adjusted if you are due to birth early (ie for a multiple birth) which is discussed upon booking or if baby makes an early appearance and I am still available for support.

Full Labour Support

Whilst midwives and doctors may have shift changes during your labour, by booking me you know you will have a constant support plus someone to discuss and advocate for your choices when there are shift changes. Leaving you (and your birth partner if you have one) to focus solely on you and your birth experience. If things deviate from your plan we can discuss options and breakdown what is comfortable for you.

2 Postnatal Visits

A chance for you to discuss your birth and where I can answer any questions you may have and/or listen to your experience. I can help with the newborn whilst you sleep, eat, connect with other children, shower or take a minute to breathe. I am also available to help with light cleaning and cooking meals whilst you spend time as a new family. I can also help with feeding support, both breast and bottle. These visits last up to 2 hours.

6 week Postnatal Phone Support

Whilst the postnatal visits are normally done in the first couple of weeks, I also offer a text or phone support for 6 weeks post birth, where you can ask me any questions or discuss anything during that time. These will be answered to as soon as I am able, or if you think you may benefit from more postnatal support visits face you face for an immediate answer these can also be booked separately for an additional charge.
