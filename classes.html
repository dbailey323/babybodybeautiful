Bumps


Wednesdays @ 6.30pm


Mountsorrel Memorial Centre

This course is for pregnant mummys looking to keep up with their fitness in a safe environment with a programme designed especially for the prenatal body. I am a fully qualified pre and postnatal fitness specialist so your workouts will all be safe throughout all stages of pregnancy.

The focus will be on keeping up fitness levels to help with labour and postnatal recovery, mobility and movement of joints to help you as your bump gets bigger and to help you lead a healthy pregnancy.

Suitable from the first to third trimester with options for all

Baby Massage


Fridays @ 10.00am

Our baby massage class designed to teach you soothing techniques to help you bond with baby and create a soothing and calming routine. This class is a 5 week course with a different body part being massaged each week.
